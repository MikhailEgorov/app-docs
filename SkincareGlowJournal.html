Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Skincare Glow Journal (“the App”) handles information when you use it. The App is developed and operated by an individual developer (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

Overview

Skincare Glow Journal is an application that allows users to track their daily nutrition and skin condition. User privacy is a core principle of the App.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information entered by the user stays entirely on their device.

Permissions

The App does not request access to the camera, storage, contacts, or any other sensitive device data.

Data Storage

All data entered into the App is stored locally on the user's device. To delete all data, users can simply uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting services.

User Identification

The App does not require account creation, registration, login, or any form of user identification.

Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all users. Since no personal data is collected, no information related to children or other users is stored or shared.

Medical Disclaimer

Skincare Glow Journal is not a medical application. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or recommendations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
