Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Chicken Swipe (“the App”) handles information when you use it. The App is developed and operated by an independent developer (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

Overview

Chicken Swipe is a casual arcade game where players control a flying chicken using swipe gestures to achieve the highest possible score. We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping the App simple and safe to use.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data. No personal information is transmitted from your device to us or to any third parties.

Permissions

The App may request permission to send local notifications. These notifications are created and handled entirely on your device and are not used to collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Game Center

Chicken Swipe uses Apple Game Center to provide leaderboards and competitive features. Game Center is operated by Apple, and any data processed through Game Center is handled according to Apple’s Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store personal information related to your Game Center account.

Data Storage

Chicken Swipe does not store user data on external servers and does not use cloud storage, backups, or remote databases.

Third-Party Services

Other than Apple Game Center, the App does not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting services.

User Accounts

The App does not require account creation, registration, login, or any form of user identification to play. Any optional Game Center participation is managed entirely through the user’s Apple ID.

Children’s Privacy

Chicken Swipe does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App itself does not collect personal data, no information from users of any age is stored or shared by us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
