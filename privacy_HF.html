Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Welcome to Mindbody Goal Tracker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Access

The App accesses health and device activity data locally on your device with your permission. We do not collect or transmit this data off your device.

  • Health Data: Steps, calories burned, and distance traveled via Apple HealthKit.
  • Screen Time Data: Device usage information via Apple Screen Time / Device Activity APIs.

Data Storage and Sharing

All data remains on your device. We do not store, back up, or share any data externally. No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking is used.

User Identification

The App does not require registration or login. You are not identified by name, email, or any unique identifier.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children, and no personal data is transmitted.

Your Control

You can manage or revoke access to Health and Screen Time data via your device settings. Revoking permissions may limit the App’s functionality.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
